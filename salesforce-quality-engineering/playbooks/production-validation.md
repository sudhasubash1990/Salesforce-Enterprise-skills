# Production Validation Playbook

## Purpose

Execute production validation / early verification after deploy.

## Participants

- Test Lead
- Release Manager
- Support

## Inputs

- Scope
- Prior artifacts

## Activities

- Prepare
- Facilitate
- Document outcomes
- Confirm next steps

## Outputs

- Meeting outcomes
- Updated artifacts

## Success Criteria

- Outcomes documented
- Owners and dates assigned
- Open risks logged

## Escalation Points

- Unresolved Critical gaps blocking design/execution
- Missing approver / environment
- Regulatory ambiguity — Legal/Compliance
