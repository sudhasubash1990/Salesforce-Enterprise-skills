# Defect Triage Playbook

## Purpose

Triage defects for severity, priority, owner, and target fix.

## Participants

- Test Lead
- Dev Lead
- BA/PO
- SA (as needed)

## Inputs

- Scope
- Prior artifacts

## Activities

- Prepare
- Facilitate
- Document outcomes
- Confirm next steps

## Outputs

- Meeting outcomes
- Updated artifacts

## Success Criteria

- Outcomes documented
- Owners and dates assigned
- Open risks logged

## Escalation Points

- Priority disputes on business impact
- Environment unavailable for retest
