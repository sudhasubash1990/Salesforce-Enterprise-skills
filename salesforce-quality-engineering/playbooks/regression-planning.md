# Regression Planning Playbook

## Purpose

Select risk-based regression scope for a release.

## Participants

- Test Lead
- BA
- SA
- Release Manager

## Inputs

- Scope
- Prior artifacts

## Activities

- Prepare
- Facilitate
- Document outcomes
- Confirm next steps

## Outputs

- In/Out/Conditional pack
- Owners

## Success Criteria

- Outcomes documented
- Owners and dates assigned
- Open risks logged

## Escalation Points

- Unresolved Critical gaps blocking design/execution
- Missing approver / environment
- Regulatory ambiguity — Legal/Compliance
