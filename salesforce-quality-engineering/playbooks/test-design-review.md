# Test Design Review Playbook

## Purpose

Peer review of scenarios/cases/coverage before execution.

## Participants

- QE peers
- BA (optional)
- Automation Lead

## Inputs

- Scope
- Prior artifacts

## Activities

- Check INVEST-like scenario quality
- Coverage gaps
- Automation candidates

## Outputs

- Meeting outcomes
- Updated artifacts

## Success Criteria

- Outcomes documented
- Owners and dates assigned
- Open risks logged

## Escalation Points

- Unresolved Critical gaps blocking design/execution
- Missing approver / environment
- Regulatory ambiguity — Legal/Compliance
