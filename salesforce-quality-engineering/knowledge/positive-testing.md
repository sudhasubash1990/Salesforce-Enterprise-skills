# Positive Testing

**Purpose:** Confirm expected behaviour with valid inputs and authorized personas.

**Scope:** Scenario objectives and design guidance only. Do **not** generate detailed test cases or automation scripts in Sprint 3.

**Owner:** QE Practice Lead

**Version:** 0.4.0

**Status:** Draft (Sprint 3)

---

## When to Use

Primary AC happy paths; smoke candidates.

## Guidance

- Cover each primary AC success path once.
- Include representative valid data, not every combination.
- Pair with negative and permission scenarios for balance.

## Scenario Objectives (Examples)

Describe **what** to verify—not step scripts.
