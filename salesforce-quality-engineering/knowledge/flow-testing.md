# Flow Testing

**Purpose:** Verify Flow entry criteria, path outcomes, fault paths, and order-of-execution impacts.

**Scope:** Scenario objectives and design guidance only. Do **not** generate detailed test cases or automation scripts in Sprint 3.

**Owner:** QE Practice Lead

**Version:** 0.4.0

**Status:** Draft (Sprint 3)

---

## When to Use

Record-triggered, screen, scheduled, orchestration Flows.

## Guidance

- Entry true/false.
- Decision outcomes.
- Fault connectors.
- Bulk behaviour risks (label assumptions).

## Scenario Objectives (Examples)

Describe **what** to verify—not step scripts.
