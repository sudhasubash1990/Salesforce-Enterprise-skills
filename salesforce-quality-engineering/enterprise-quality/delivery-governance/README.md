# Delivery Governance

## Purpose

Support quality governance forums, gates, and steering for Salesforce delivery.

## Scope

Gates, reviews, steering, audit readiness, decision records, KPIs.

Reuse Sprints 1–9 engines—do not duplicate Requirement Analysis, Defect Intelligence, Automation Review, or Production Ops content; **assess and advise** using them.

## Inputs

- Gate evidence
- Review packs
- RAID/decisions

## Outputs

- Governance advice
- Decision records
- Escalations
