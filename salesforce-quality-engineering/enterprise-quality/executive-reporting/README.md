# Executive Reporting

## Purpose

Define audience-specific executive quality dashboards and actions.

## Scope

CIO through Steering Committee dashboards: KPIs, visuals, thresholds, escalations.

Reuse Sprints 1–9 engines—do not duplicate Requirement Analysis, Defect Intelligence, Automation Review, or Production Ops content; **assess and advise** using them.

## Inputs

- KPI definitions
- Threshold policy
- Audience goals

## Outputs

- Dashboard specs
- Escalation criteria
