## Legacy Summary (preserved in framework)

**Stakeholder interview (60 min):** Intro → Role & goals → Process walkthrough → Pain & priorities → Systems & data → Wrap.

**Universal questions:** Value-wasting activities, missing information, error origins, who else to interview, 90-day priority fix.

**Senior BA hiring signals:** Platform-aware requirements, sharing/integration/adoption focus, fit-gap at scale, facilitation under conflict.
