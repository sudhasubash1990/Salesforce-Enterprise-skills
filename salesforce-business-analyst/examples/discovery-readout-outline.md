# Worked Example: Discovery Readout Outline

Sample structure for discovery phase close-out presentation.

## Slide / Section Outline

### 1. Engagement Recap

- Objectives from charter
- Timeline and team

### 2. Current State Summary

- AS-IS process highlights (1 diagram)
- Systems landscape
- Top 5 pain points (validated)

### 3. Proposed TO-BE Capabilities

| Capability | Priority | Release hint |
|------------|----------|--------------|
| Unified Account from ERP | Must | R1 |
| Dealer portal cases | Must | R3 |
| Mobile activity logging | Should | R1 |

### 4. Scope Recommendation

**In:** Sales Cloud core, Service cases, ERP Account sync, dealer portal MVP

**Out:** Marketing Cloud, CPQ (phase 2)

### 5. RAID Summary

- Top 3 risks with mitigation
- Assumptions needing validation by date
- Critical dependencies (ERP API availability)

### 6. Next Steps

- Fit-gap workshop week of [date]
- BRD draft v0.5 by [date]
- Architect integration deep-dive

### 7. Decisions Needed Today

1. Portal platform confirmation
2. SSO approach for dealers
3. Release 1 go-live date commitment

## Facilitator Notes

- Timebox decisions; log unresolved as open questions
- Get explicit sponsor verbal alignment on out-of-scope items
