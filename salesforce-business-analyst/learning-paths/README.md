## How to Use

1. Identify your level using [ba-maturity-model.md](../ba-maturity-model.md) Section 4
2. Complete the reading list in order for your level
3. Complete practice exercises with a mentor or AI coach
4. Pass the exit checklist before advancing
5. Use [interview-guide/interview-index.md](../interview-guide/interview-index.md) for assessment prep
