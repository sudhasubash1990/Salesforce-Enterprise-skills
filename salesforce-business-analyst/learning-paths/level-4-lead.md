## Practice Exercises

1. **BRD approval review** — Conduct a quality review of a team BRD using checklists and validation framework
2. **Certification assessment** — Complete a certification report for an L2 candidate using the template
3. **Benchmark facilitation** — Run benchmark scenario RH-03 (story pack validation) with a mentee
4. **Repository validation** — Execute `python scripts/validate_repository.py` and remediate findings
5. **Steering pack** — Prepare a steering committee deck for a portfolio risk escalation

## Exit Checklist

- [ ] Establishes BA quality standards across a delivery unit
- [ ] Mentors L1–L3 analysts using learning paths and interview guide
- [ ] Approves major deliverables (BRD, FRD, solution recommendations)
- [ ] Runs enterprise validation and certification processes
- [ ] Influences portfolio-level decisions and governance
- [ ] Passes L4 interview topics: [interview-guide/advanced/executive-communication.md](../interview-guide/advanced/executive-communication.md)
