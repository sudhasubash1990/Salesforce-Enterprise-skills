## Practice Exercises

1. **Fit-gap analysis** — Classify 10 capabilities as Standard/Config/Extend/Gap for a healthcare scenario
2. **Solution options** — Present three solution options with trade-offs for an integration requirement
3. **Executive summary** — Write a one-page recommendation for steering committee
4. **Capability assessment** — Map business capabilities to Salesforce clouds for a banking scenario
5. **Validation pass** — Review a draft BRD against brain validation framework and checklists

## Exit Checklist

- [ ] Leads enterprise workshops with complex stakeholders
- [ ] Applies decision framework consistently (config before custom)
- [ ] Produces executive-ready recommendations with explicit assumptions
- [ ] Validates AI-generated content before client delivery
- [ ] Influences solution direction on medium-to-large programs
- [ ] Passes L3 interview topics: [interview-guide/advanced/scenario-questions.md](../interview-guide/advanced/scenario-questions.md)
