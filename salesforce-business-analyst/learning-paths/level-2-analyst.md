## Practice Exercises

1. **BRD section** — Produce a complete business requirements section for a utilities customer onboarding scenario
2. **AS-IS / TO-BE** — Map current and future state for a retail order fulfilment process
3. **RTM starter** — Link 5 business requirements to user stories in an RTM
4. **Discovery workshop** — Facilitate a mock discovery using the discovery playbook agenda
5. **MoSCoW prioritization** — Prioritize a backlog of 15 requirements with rationale

## Exit Checklist

- [ ] Independently facilitates requirement workshops
- [ ] Produces BRD/FRD sections meeting [checklists.md](../checklists.md) gates
- [ ] Performs basic gap analysis between AS-IS and TO-BE
- [ ] Applies industry scenario context to requirements
- [ ] Maintains RTM from business requirement to story
- [ ] Passes L2 interview topics: [interview-guide/requirement-gathering.md](../interview-guide/requirement-gathering.md)
