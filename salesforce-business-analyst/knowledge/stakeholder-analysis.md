# Stakeholder Analysis

## Overview

Stakeholder identification, power-interest analysis, RACI, and communication planning.

## Purpose

Ensure the right voices are heard, decisions have owners, and communication matches audience.

## Why It Matters

Missing stakeholders surface late as blockers; unclear RACI causes sign-off delays.

## Business Context

Salesforce programs touch sales, service, IT, compliance, channels, and executives.

## Salesforce Context

Include admins, architects, integration teams, and Experience Cloud business owners early.

## Core Concepts

**Power-interest matrix:**

| | Low interest | High interest |
|---|-------------|---------------|
| **High power** | Keep satisfied | Manage closely |
| **Low power** | Monitor | Keep informed |

**RACI:** Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed per process or deliverable.

**Communication plan:** Audience, message, channel, frequency.

## Key Terminology

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Sponsor | Executive funding and escalation |
| PO | Backlog priority and acceptance |

## Enterprise Best Practices

- Map stakeholders in week 1 of discovery
- Engage likely dissenters before final sign-off
- Single accountable name per decision

## Common Mistakes

- IT-only discovery
- RACI with teams not people for Accountable

## Anti-Patterns

- Stakeholder map never updated after org change

## Decision Guidelines

Escalate to sponsor when Accountable role vacant for Must decisions.

## Industry Considerations

Public sector: public comment periods and policy offices as consulted stakeholders.
